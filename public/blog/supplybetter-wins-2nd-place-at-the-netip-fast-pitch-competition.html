SupplyBetter wins 2nd place at the NetIP Fast Pitch Competition

We're proud to report that SupplyBetter recently placed 2nd at the NetIP Fast Pitch Competition here in San Francisco. We had the pleasure of competing against companies working on solving some really interesting problems such as Airpost (1st place) and CampBX (3rd Place). Thank you NetIP for letting us compete and Rackspace for sponsoring the event. With our 2nd place standing we've won $1000/month credit on the Rackspace US Cloud portfolio for 12 months. We're excited to make the switch from Heroku to Rackspace, and we'll be sure to let you know how switching goes.

Cheers,

Rob
